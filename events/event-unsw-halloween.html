// Operation Briefing
Transmitting live from the UNSW Roundhouse. Fusing high-energy J-Rock riffs, sweeping mecha strings, and gothic classics, this monumental stage marked the final live performance with Peter, our very first violinist.

Our performance at the UNSW Roundhouse represented a highly emotional university stage deployment. In addition to delivering an explosive 12-song set list ranging from *Bocchi the Rock!* to *Slam Dunk*, the show served as a special farewell tribute to Peter, !nvolution's founding violinist, marking his final live transmission with the band.

Deploying a massive triple-threat block of Kalafina mecha symphonies (*Sprinter*, *Magia*, and *To the Beginning*) alongside guest vocal sequences, the performance compiled with supreme precision, honoring Peter's legacy and mapping an unforgettable experience onto the campus crowd.
