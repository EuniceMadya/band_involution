// The Concept

Not Your Involution

Breaking free from the competitive loop.

In modern sociology, involution represents the exhausting social rat race—where individuals face hyper-intense competition for stagnant resources, leading to overwork, burnout, and anxiety without real personal progress.

We prefix our name with the programming symbol ! (which represents the logical NOT operator) to negate it. !nvolution is our creative escape. By merging energetic J-Rock guitar riffs, sparkling synthesizers, and clean clean vocals, we create a high-power sonic refuge to pull you out of the daily grind.
